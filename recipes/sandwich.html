SANDVICH

Edible device

User manual:

  • Bread - of your taste;

  • Veggie group - salad slice, 2 tomatoes slices and olive on top!(that matters!);

  • Cheese - the more - the better;

  • Meat group - slice of ham, bacon is optional;

  • Sauce group - mayo, ketchup, anything will work.

How to make:

  1. Cook the bacon - until crisp;

  2. Toast the bread - toast and spread slices with a sauce;

  3. Add layers - add all the ingredients as you'd like;

  4. Olive on top - don't forget to pierce your sandvich and place olive on top!

  5. Eat - to regain up to 300 health.
